About Digital Lakes

The Digital Lakes project is part of Egypt's digital transformation strategy. It aims to monitor Egyptian lakes using smart sensors that collect environmental data such as water quality, temperature, and pollution levels in real time. This helps improve decision-making, protect marine life, and ensure sustainable water management.



By combining technology and sustainability, Digital Lakes represents a step toward a smarter, greener Egypt. The collected data will support scientists and decision-makers to keep our natural resources healthy for future generations.
